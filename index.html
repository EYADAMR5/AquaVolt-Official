The Mechanism

01. Hydro Intake

Water from irrigation canals enters the screw system using natural gravity flow, requiring no external pumps.

02. Mechanical Rotation

The water pressure turns the lightweight polymer screw, converting kinetic energy into torque.

03. Electrical Generation

A high-performance alternator converts rotation into clean electricity for immediate use or grid supply.
