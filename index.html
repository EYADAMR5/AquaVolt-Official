How It Works

1. Water Capture

Water from irrigation canals enters the screw system with natural flow pressure.

2. Kinetic Conversion

The Archimedes screw rotates as water passes, converting kinetic energy to mechanical force.

3. Power Generation

The rotation drives a high-efficiency generator to produce 24/7 clean electricity.
