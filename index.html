Our Vision

AquaVolt is an innovative initiative from STEM El Sadat City students, aiming to revolutionize hydro-energy in Egypt. Our goal is to provide local farms and irrigation networks with zero-carbon electricity using non-iron, polymer turbines.

Why Archimedes?

Our screw-design turbine works perfectly with low-head water flow, making it ideal for Egypt's vast irrigation canals.
