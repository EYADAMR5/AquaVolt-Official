OUR MISSION

Sustainable Energy for Every Farm

AquaVolt is a student-led innovation from STEM El Sadat City, focused on solving the energy crisis in rural areas. Our Archimedes Screw Turbine is designed to be cost-effective, durable, and environmentally friendly.

How It Works

01. Intake

Water from canals enters the turbine screw naturally.

02. Rotation

The polymer screw rotates, generating mechanical torque.

03. Output

Electricity is produced and ready for agricultural use.
